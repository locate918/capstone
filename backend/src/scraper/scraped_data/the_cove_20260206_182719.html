The Cove at River Spirit Casino Info

            
The River Spirit Casino is a casino and resort located in Tulsa, OK. It contains the Paradise Cove, an entertainment venue which hosts events including rock concerts, comedy, MMA and boxing. It is just the right size to accommodate larger acts but still provide an intimate fan experience. The Casino also offers live music at its Margaritaville Restaurant. Those visiting the casino for a show can also take advantage of the many amenities the resort has to offer.
